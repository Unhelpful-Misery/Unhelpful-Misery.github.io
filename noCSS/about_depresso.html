I am Depresso*. I've kind of made it my life goal to never be boring!

I personally think the best way to start getting to know people is by looking at the things they have done, which is why I highly recommend you start out by checking out the projects I've made! I think of making projects as a big part of who I am.

Besides my projects, I'm really interested in mathematics and physics. As a result of those two interests, I happen to be somewhat interested in computer science and coding as well, just because computers turn out to be incredibly useful in both math(s) and physics!

If you don't know me too well, you might think I'm a high-tech kind of person, but I would argue that's only half correct! I really enjoy having my Light Phone, which is basically a super-dumbed down phone that can't access the internet. And although I do have tons of programs running on my computer at any given moment, most of those programs just block me from accessing other technologies, like blocking short-form content and limiting the amount of YouTube I can watch in a given session. Without all of this, I really don't think I could've created all of the projects that I've created so far!

Yet, I've still been using Linux for a considerable length of time, I carry around my Eraser Holder, and I do spend many weekends obsessing over programs and spreadsheet formulas, so I would personally say I'm in a weird situation of being simultaneously high-tech and low-tech.

Anyway, when I do decide I deserve a bit of a break from working, I do still like to relax and watch anime or play games. My favourite anime for a while has been Girls' Last Tour but I do also enjoy any anime with a really good/compelling story. In terms of games, I can really get into super difficult games that make you feel an amazing sense of accomplishment once you finally finish them, which is why I've collected my Celeste moments and Touhou moments!
