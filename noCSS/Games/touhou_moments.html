Thoughts

I know bullet hells aren't for everybody, but I personally love the Touhou games I've played so far. There's just something so amazing about that moment when you perfectly dodge and weave through an attack that you initially thought was impossible. That being said, I'm still a terrible Touhou player, though.

Touhou 7: Perfect Cherry Blossom

I personally believe that PCB is the best beginner Touhou game, excluding how difficult it is to actually obtain the game. Maybe I'm just saying that because it was the first Touhou game I played, though. In any case, even though it's really easy, PCB's also really fun and the attack patterns are super pretty. I think PCB has a special place in a lot of people's hearts and it definitely has a special place in mine.


Marisa might not be the optimal character to play, but I just love her MASTER SPARK!!







I really did not expect to beat the Extra Stage on this run, to be completely honest.

Touhou 8: Imperishable Night

I'll be honest, I never really understood the "Time" mechanic really well - sometimes I think I got a lot of score points and it advances by a full hour, and other times I think I did horribly and the time only advances 30 minutes. Anyway, I've learned to mostly ignore the time when playing my runs, since I don't use continues anyway. The best part about Imperishable Night has got to be the fight against Marisa. There's something so awesome about having to dodge Master Spark. Although, I will say, all of the boss fights are made significantly easier when you have, like, 2 business days to death bomb if you're playing as Reimu.

Touhou 10: Mountain of Faith

It took a WHILE to get adjusted to the fact that bombing meant my power went down, but I slowly started to realise how generous Touhou 10 is with giving you power items. Kanako seems really difficult at first, but once you start understanding her attacks, they become actually pretty fun. (except for her last one).



Once again, a character that can just put a bomb right in the enemy's face. That'll never get old.

Touhou 13: Ten Desires

Ten Desires was interesting to play through. I really liked the music during some sections and the attacks were generally really pretty too. Overall, though, I feel like Ten Desires was just another game in the series, and wasn't really too standout? Maybe that's just because the main gimmick wasn't really that interesting, although PCB's gimmick isn't really that interesting either.

I will say, however, Youmu's attack is *really* fun to wield.


Compared to all of the attacks I've seen so far, Youmu's attack is so interesting. It has to be the coolest thus far.




No, I will not shut up about how cool Youmu's attack is.
