Thoughts

I'm sure many people already know this by now, but Celeste is truly such a wonderful game. When I heard from multiple people how amazing Celeste was, I tried watching a playthrough but was ultimately bored and thought that Celeste wasn't for me. It took me a while to give Celeste another try - actually playing it this time - and now I realise that Celeste is one of those games where *you* have to be the one who's playing it for the magic to really settle in. Anyway, you might be asking why I would then put videos of my Celeste gameplay on my own website, given my belief that you have to play Celeste yourself to truely experience it. My response to that is that, yeah, you're right. This page is mostly just for myself to look back upon and feel satisfied at my previous accomplishments. I don't really anticipate other people will find this page too interesting.

If you're here anyway, I formatted most of these videos to include 1 death at the start before I clear the level. I like to think of it as a nice reminder of how experiencing failure is a necessary part of succeeding.
