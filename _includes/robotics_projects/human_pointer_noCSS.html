Human Pointer

Time I spent making this: 1061 minutes

Money I spent making this: $173.56

A fun little Human Pointer robot I created, just because I wanted a fully autonomous device to accompany my Eraser Holder!

Despite being its creator it has terrified me with two features that I didn't know it had.

  1. It can see through walls.
  2. It spasms like a trapped animal if you try to turn it off, even though I should be it its blind spot when deactivating it.
