Daily Checklist Project

This is a simple program that my sister and I collaborated to created! It notifies you of any chores that you should remember today (like taking out the trash, doing laundry, etc). It displays both the items that should be done today, as well as the items that are overdue (weren't completed when they were supposed to). To clear an item, just type in its name!

I find this incredibly helpful for making sure I'm on top of all of those necessary but easy-to-forget tasks. You can download the three main files for the program below! (Change the contents of “Checklist Data.txt” to whatever suits your needs.)

Entertainment Limiting Script

I made this script in collaboration with my sister to limit the amount of time I can spend on entertainment sites! If you try to spend more than 50 minutes on an entertainment site (currently just youtube.com, but you can add others!), the script will automatically kick you out, and you’ll have to wait 60 minutes before you can do entertainment again. The specific time intervals are also adjustable to whatever your heart desires! I highly recommend pairing this script with a basic website blocker plugin.
